Favorite Books

Elon Musk

Author: Walter Isaacson

Elon does more to promote humanity than anyone in the world. Renewable energy, space travel, and free speech are all huge net positive brought upon the world by him, yet he is seemingly criticized and attacked more than anybody. Based human.

Sapiens

Author: Yuval Noah Harari

Sapiens showed me all the default programming I had around thinking humans are the most important thing on earth, when considering the length of time earth has been around, humans have barely been and will eventually cease to be around, just as 99.9% of the species before it have.

Models

Author: Mark Manson

Models helped me be me, because the most attractive version of myself to others is the most authentic one.

Lying

Author: Sam Harris

Lying, even in small ways, corrodes relationships and societal trust. We should all be more radically honest.

Factfulness

Author: Hans Rosling

If for nothing else than showing me how people use data to manipulate our perspectives and serve their narratives, factfulness is good for cutting through emotional statements about the world and giving you data to think about it.

Calypso

Author: David Sedaris

Calypso is laugh out loud funny, just read it.
